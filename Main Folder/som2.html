Food And Mood

Take Small Steps:

Making changes can be really tough – especially if you’re feeling low. You may not be able to change your whole diet suddenly. Try step by step.
You might not feel better right away, and there might be times where you feel frustrated. But try to keep going! But Remember! Small changes can make a difference in the long term.

Keep a food diary:

Write down what you eat and make notes.
Work out how particular items effect you:keep you awake or help you sleep
make you feel better or worse

Manage food intolerances:

Intolerances to particular foods (such as wheat, dairy or yeast) can cause lots of unpleasant feelings, both physical and mental. If you’re concerned about this, ask a health professional to help you investigate your tolerances safely. See NHS Choices for more information about food intolerances.
